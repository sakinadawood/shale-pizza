A Fine dining experience!

Established for more than 25 years, we are proud to be one of the must-sees of Old Hull. We make it our duty to make each of your visits a pleasant event for your reunion of all kinds. Whether at lunchtime or in the evening, we seek to challenge your senses to nourish your belly and your mind.

At Piz'za-za, the primary objective is to prepare and serve you the best plate, composed as much as possible of local products. Added to this is our selection of privately imported wines carefully chosen to titillate and satisfy your taste buds.

It's in the heart of our Downtown where it's great to eat that we hope to see you again and again.
